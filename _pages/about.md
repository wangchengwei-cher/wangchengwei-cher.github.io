I am Chengwei Wang (王程伟), and you can also call me Cher. I am an Assistant Professor from University of International Business and Economics. I received my Joint Ph.D. degrees from Renmin University of China and City University of Hong Kong. My research lies in public administration and public policy, with a focus on performance feedback, strategic behavior, and governance under pressure-driven systems.
