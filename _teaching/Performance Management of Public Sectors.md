The course "Performance Management of Public Sectors" is designed for international master students, aiming to develop high-level professionals who can engage in performance management and evaluation within government and nonprofit organizations. With public administration shifting towards precision, data-driven decision-making, and performance-oriented governance, this course equips students with the theoretical framework, core tools, and practical applications needed to enhance their expertise in policymaking, organizational management, and performance assessment.
